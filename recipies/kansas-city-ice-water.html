Kansas City Ice Water

  • 2 cups ice, or as needed
  • 1 fluid ounce gin
  • 1 fluid ounce vodka
  • 1/2 fluid ounce lime juice
  • 1/2 fluid oucnce triple sec
  • 3 fluid ounces lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
  1. Fill a shaker with ice; add gin, vodka, lime juice, and triple sec. Cover and shake until the outside of the shaker has frosted. Strain into a chilled pint glass with ice.
  2. Pour in lemon-lime soda to serve.
